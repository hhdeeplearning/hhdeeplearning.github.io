Deep Learning Course at Halmstad University

This course is taught in the School of Information Technology at Halmstad University. The aim of this course is to introduce students to the fundamentals of deep learning and develop practical skills to implement various deep neural network based solutions for typical problems in machine learning and data mining. The course covers both theoretical and practical aspects of deep neural networks. The course is taught by Professor Mattias Ohlsson and Assistant Professor Eren Erdal Aksoy.
